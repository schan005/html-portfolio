The Best Movies of All Time According to Sarah

My top 3 favorite movies of all time!

Edward Scissorhands

A great movie that shows the juxtaposition of conformity of social norms and understanding those who don't follow convention. Be your unique self!
In addition to "What's Eating Gilbert Grape", this is the only other Johnny Depp movie that I can watch.

Spirited Away

A Ghibli animation with beautiful imagery and story telling that brings elements of Japanese history and culture to a modern set story.
Studio Ghibili animations are some of the best in the world, and the stories are able to interpret Japanese culture and history in very interesting ways.

Goodbye Lenin

Beautiful storytelling of a part of post war Germany that intertwines a family and love story with political and historical changes in Germany.
